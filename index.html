‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to register a business in SA?

+

Sole Proprietor: R0 (free)
Pty (Ltd): R175 for online registration via CIPC
Close Corporation (CC): No longer available for new registrations

Additional costs may include: Name reservation (R50), Company Secretary (optional), Registered office address.

Do I need a business bank account?

+

Yes! It's essential for:

  • Separating personal and business finances
  • Tax compliance and record-keeping
  • Professional credibility
  • Easier accounting and bookkeeping
Most banks require: Company registration cert, ID, proof of address, and R10-R100 deposit.

When should I register for VAT?

+

Mandatory: When your annual turnover exceeds R1 million
Voluntary: You can register before R1m if it benefits your business

Benefits: Claim VAT back on business expenses
Responsibilities: Charge 15% VAT, submit bi-monthly returns, maintain records

How do I get funding without collateral?

+

Options for funding without collateral:

  • SEDA: Free training + potential funding
  • NEF: Focuses on black-owned businesses
  • Angel Investors: Equity-based funding
  • Crowdfunding: Platforms like Thr Funding
  • Business Competitions: SAB Kickstart, etc.
Key: Strong business plan, financial projections, market research!
