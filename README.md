# Small-Business-Assistant
Small Business Assistant helps entrepreneurs in South Africa get the right channels, use AI assistant for recommendations and sources, and steps on how to grow their business using the right SA channels and comply with regulations.

## Features
* Find right channels for SA businesses
* AI recommendations for business growth
* Resources for SA business regulations compliance
* Links to SA government resources, local business networks, and popular business tools

## Resources
* SA government websites (e.g., Companies and Intellectual Property Commission)
* Local business networks (e.g., Business chambers, industry associations)
* Popular business tools (e.g., accounting software, marketing platforms)
